Research areas

The deformation of fluid interfaces is ubiquitous in nature, such as during bubble or droplet coalescence, pinch-off, oscillation. My research interest is on studying particle-laden fluid interface dynamics using high speed photography and image analysis, with a focus on interficial tension effect. The insight from our work enhances fundamental understanding of the stability of particle-laden foams and emulsions.
